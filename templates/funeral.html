    Pet Funeral

    “Our team understands the pain of loss and ensures to provide you with all the support and best of services.”

    FamilyDog believes that our pets should receive the same respect as humans in all spheres of life even during the ultimate goodbye. To see your pet take his or her last breath in front of you is the most painful feeling. It becomes more difficult when we don’t have enough information about the funeral rites of our pets. Our founder realized this pain during the demise of his best friend's pet “Lucy”. Despite working in the pet sector he was clueless as the spot they know for burial was under construction. Later on, while working on this project, we realized and asked our team that “Is this the pet funeral we can give to our pets who lived with us more years which covers a plethora of memories. Is it justified to dump them in a pit without anything of their remembrance? ”


    Deceased Pet Pickup

    We provide transport service either from a veterinary clinic or your home. Depending upon the distance, your deceased pet would be picked up as soon as possible. We cover the whole Delhi and outskirts of Delhi as well to limited pin codes of Gurgaon and Noida.




    Carcass Disposal

    The most appropriate method would be suggested to the pet parents according to the nearest carcass disposal service available. Pet parents can choose either burial or incineration as the mode of carcass disposal for their pets. Pet parents can take ashes with themselves to scatter them in the holy river of Ganga.


    Religious Ceremony Of Deceased Pet

    Either Birth or Death marks the most important events. It is just the disposal of the body not the soul like an individual changes clothes. The religious ceremony is performed by a priest to facilitate the last rites a soul deserves.


    Pet Memorabilia

    Whether it be a paw print on a cement tile, a headstone, or even an urn, we got you covered. We can try our best to provide anything as per the request as well. We are continuously making efforts to expand this segment and provide a product that pet parents can cherish throught the life in remembrance of their pets.
