    About Us

    FamilyDog is a project started by Prince Gulati, as a Delhi Technological University student in his 6th semester. The project grabbed his attention due to his immense love for his dog Rio and the lack of information available in society. FamilyDog has served well its clients including some famous personalities for more than three until Prince decided to upgrade his management knowledge and went to Europe for his further education. During his time in Europe, he saw a different kind of pet sector with a different level of breeding, an inclination of pet owners towards organic food for pets, and equal importance. He met various breeders, understood various processes, and explored amazing natural products. He had to come back to India in June 2020 via Vande Bharat Mission from Stockholm, Sweden where he was working as a Business Analyst. Putting everything together and taking it further, FamilyDog 2021 is reborn.

    Our Mission

    To provide Dignified Funeral Services, Organic Products, and Reduce the Mortality Rate of Puppies.

    Our Plan

    To Work with Ethical Breeders, source Organic Ingredients from the world, and Deliver End-2-End Pet Funeral Solutions.

    Our Vision

    Our Vision thrives to add Value to Society and give our Amazing Pets the Best of Products and Services they Deserve.
